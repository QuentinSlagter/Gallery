Wonderful sight of Space!
I am an astronomer and spend my time identifying interstellar medium shells (IMS). Using the WIYN 0.9-m telescope at Kitt Peak National Observatory, we revceive high angular resolution H-alpha maps of the ionized hydrogen in these shells. In simplest terms, we are looking for hot gas and dust that takes the shape of a bubble. Now when I got my painting, I was on top of the world. I love looking at nebulas and the stars! This painting perfectly encaptulates the awe that I feel every day looking through my telescope. I highly recommend this company to all who see this.
